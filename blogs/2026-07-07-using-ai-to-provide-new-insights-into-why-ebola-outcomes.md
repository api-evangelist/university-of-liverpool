University of Liverpool researchers have led two major new studies into Ebola virus disease (EVD) and factors that influence and predict patient outcomes. There is currently a serious outbreak in the Democratic Republic of Congo of the severe and often fatal illness. Symptoms include fever, vomiting, diarrhoea, severe dehydration and haemorrhage.
